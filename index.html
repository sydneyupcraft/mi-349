Downeast Maine Pumpkin Bread

Laurie Fontaine Bennett
Mar 3, 2023

During the cold and rainy season, or during the spooky time of Halloween, this recipe is sure to warm you up!

Prep Time: 15 minutes ~ Cook Time: 50 mins ~ Total Time: 1 hr 5 mins ~ Yield: 2 Loaves

Pumpkin anything is a hit during the fall season, and severely underutilized during the rainy season. This bread has been a hit with my roommates and is sure to warm up a home in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 15 ounce can pumpkin puree
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
